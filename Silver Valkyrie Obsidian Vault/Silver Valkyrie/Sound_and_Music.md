# Sound and Music

Audio direction for Silver Valkyrie—covering music, sound effects, and voice acting.

---

## Audio Philosophy

Silver Valkyrie's audio should feel like a **16-bit game with modern production quality**. Think the memorable melodies and clear sound design of Genesis/SNES era games, but with fuller frequency range and better mixing.

Every sound serves two purposes:
1. **Feedback**: Tells the player something happened
2. **Immersion**: Reinforces the Norse warrior fantasy

---

## Music Direction

### Overall Style

**Primary Influence**: Heavy metal meets chiptune/16-bit orchestral

**Reference Points**:
- Devil's Crush soundtrack (dark, driving, memorable)
- Castlevania series (gothic orchestral metal)
- Shovel Knight (modern chiptune with punch)
- Doom (2016) - for intensity reference

**Instrumentation Mix**:
- Crunchy rhythm guitars
- Epic orchestral elements (horns, strings)
- Driving drums (double bass for intensity)
- Synth/chiptune elements for retro flavor
- Nordic folk instruments (optional accents: Hardanger fiddle, tagelharpa)

### Track List

| Track | Usage | Mood | Length |
|-------|-------|------|--------|
| **Main Theme** | Title screen, attract mode | Epic, inviting | 2-3 min loop |
| **Battle Standard** | Normal gameplay | Driving, focused | 3-4 min loop |
| **Frozen Fury** | Ice Trolls mode | Cold, aggressive | 1-2 min loop |
| **Ogre's Rage** | Giant Ogre mode | Heavy, brutal | 1-2 min loop |
| **Rise of the Dead** | Draugr Horde mode | Eerie, building | 2 min loop |
| **Berserker** | Berserk Multiball | Intense, chaotic | 1 min loop |
| **The Feast** | Feast Hurry-Up | Triumphant, urgent | 30 sec loop |
| **Valhalla Awaits** | Master Slayer mode | Ultimate epic | 2-3 min |
| **Victory Fanfare** | Mode completion | Triumphant | 10-15 sec |
| **Defeat Sting** | Ball drain | Somber, brief | 3-5 sec |
| **Game Over** | Final ball drain | Reflective | 15-20 sec |
| **High Score** | New high score | Celebratory | 20-30 sec |

### Music Implementation Notes

**Transitions**:
- Mode music should fade in quickly when mode starts
- Return to main theme should feel natural, not jarring
- Multiball should layer on top of or replace current track
- Wizard mode builds from quiet to overwhelming

**Intensity Scaling** (Optional/Advanced):
- Music could react to gameplay intensity
- More instruments/layers as score multiplier increases
- Tempo could slightly increase during hurry-ups

## Sound Effects

### Flipper Sounds

| Action | Sound | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Flipper activate | Crisp "thwack" | Immediate, satisfying |
| Flipper hit ball | Solid "clack" | Varies by ball speed |
| Flipper release | Subtle "click" | Quieter than activate |

### Target Sounds

| Element | Sound | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Drop target hit | Metallic "clunk" + shatter | Ice crack for TROLL targets |
| Drop target reset | Mechanical "chunk-chunk-chunk" | Sequential, satisfying |
| Pop bumper hit | Punchy "boing" + impact | Quick, not annoying |
| Slingshot hit | Sharp "crack" | Metal on metal |
| Spinner rotation | Rising "whirr" | Pitch increases with speed |
| Saucer capture | "Thunk" + magical chime | Ball entering cave |
| Saucer eject | "Whoosh" + release | Ball launching out |
| Scoop capture | Deep "clunk" | Wooden/hall reverb |
| Scoop eject | "Thoom" + release | Powerful launch |

### Bash Target (Ogre) Sounds

| Action | Sound | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Normal hit | Heavy "THUD" + grunt | Meaty impact |
| Stun hit | "CRACK" + dazed sound | More intense |
| Killing blow | Wet "SCHLICK" + death cry | Satisfying violence |

### Scoring Sounds

| Event | Sound | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Points awarded | Subtle "ding" or "pip" | Shouldn't be annoying |
| Big points (100K+) | Rising chime | More significant |
| Jackpot | Triumphant flourish | Unmistakable |
| Multiplier activate | Power-up sound | "SLAY complete" feel |
| Combo chain | Escalating tone | Higher pitch each hit |

### Ball Sounds

| Event | Sound | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Ball launch | "Whoosh" + plunger release | Satisfying departure |
| Ball rolling | Subtle rumble | Very quiet, ambient |
| Ball save | "SAVED!" alert + magical catch | Relief sound |
| Ball drain | Descending tone + "clunk" | Defeat, but not harsh |

### Mode Sounds

| Event | Sound | Notes |
|-------|-------|-------|
| Mode qualified | Alert chime + voice | "ICE TROLLS READY" |
| Mode start | Dramatic hit + music change | Big moment |
| Mode objective | Checkpoint sound | Progress indicator |
| Mode victory | Fanfare + voice | Triumph |
| Mode failure | Descending tone | Disappointment, not punishment |

### Ambient/Environmental

| Element | Sound | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Ice Trolls area | Cold wind, ice cracking | During mode especially |
| Ogre area | Grunting, breathing | Creature presence |
| Draugr mode | Moaning, rattling bones | Creepy atmosphere |
| Valhalla | Heavenly choir, thunder | Divine presence |

## Voice Acting

### Sasha the Silver Valkyrie

**Voice Direction**: Opera-level theatrical delivery. Think Xena meets Wagner. Confident, powerful, never apologetic. Every line should sell the fantasy completely.

**Casting Notes**:
- Strong mezzo-soprano or alto
- Theater/opera training preferred
- Comfortable with battle cries and intense delivery
- Can do both commanding and victorious tones

**Line Categories**:

#### Battle Cries (During gameplay)
- *"HAVE AT THEE!"*
- *"FOR VALHALLA!"*
- *"FEEL MY BLADE!"*
- *"STAND AND FIGHT!"*

#### Mode Start Lines
- *"The trolls will FALL!"* (Ice Trolls)
- *"Face me, BEAST!"* (Giant Ogre)
- *"Return to your GRAVES!"* (Draugr)
- *"UNLEASH THE FURY!"* (Berserk)
- *"To VICTORY!"* (Feast)
- *"VALHALLA AWAITS!"* (Wizard Mode)

#### Victory Lines
- *"SASHA SLAYS!"*
- *"Back to the pit!"*
- *"Thou shall curse this land NO MORE!"*
- *"Another victory for the VALKYRIE!"*

#### Wizard Mode Lines
- *"The gates open only for the WORTHY!"*
- *"THE MASTER SLAYER RISES!"*
- *"ETERNAL GLORY!"*

#### Utility Lines
- *"Well struck!"* (Jackpot)
- *"The fury grows!"* (Multiplier up)
- *"Odin watches!"* (Ball save)
- *"I shall return..."* (Game over)

### Enemy Voices

#### Ice Trolls
**Voice Direction**: Gravelly, hungry, predatory. Like a creature that's been eating people for centuries and enjoys it.

**Lines**:
- *"Tonight we dine on Viking Soup!"*
- *"Person-meat! My favorite!"*
- *"Tiny warrior... BIG meal!"*
- *"We'll pick our teeth with your bones!"*
- *"ARRRGH! You'll pay for that!"* (wounded)
- *"NOOO! Sister/Brother!"* (death)

#### Giant Ogre
**Voice Direction**: Deep, stupid, overconfident. A brute who's never been challenged until now.

**Lines**:
- *"I'll crush you like a WALNUT!"*
- *"Stand STILL, little bug!"*
- *"OOF!"* / *"GAH!"* / *"STOP THAT!"* (hit reactions)
- *"My head... it's SPINNING..."* (stunned)
- *"Noooo! My beautiful FACE!"* (death)

#### Draugr
**Voice Direction**: Not individual voices—ambient moaning, rattling, otherworldly whispers. The horror is in the mass, not individuals.

### System Voice (Optional)

A narrator voice for UI/system announcements. Could be:
- **Odin**: Deep, godly, watching from above
- **Neutral Announcer**: Classic pinball callout style
- **Sasha herself**: First-person callouts

**Example Lines**:
- *"MULTIBALL!"*
- *"EXTRA BALL!"*
- *"JACKPOT!"*
- *"Player One... GAME OVER."*

## Audio Implementation Notes

### Priority System

Not all sounds should play at once. Priority hierarchy:

1. **Voice lines** (never cut off mid-word)
2. **Mode music**
3. **Major scoring events** (jackpots, mode completion)
4. **Flipper/ball sounds**
5. **Target hits**
6. **Ambient sounds**

### Mixing Guidelines

- Music should duck slightly during voice lines
- Flipper sounds should always be audible
- Pop bumper rapid hits should not become overwhelming
- Master volume and category volumes in options menu

### Audio Options (Player-Facing)

- Master Volume
- Music Volume
- Sound Effects Volume
- Voice Volume
- Mono/Stereo toggle

## Music Production Notes (For Composer)

### Deliverables Needed

- Stems (separate tracks for guitars, drums, synths, etc.)
- Loop points clearly marked
- Multiple intensity versions if possible
- 44.1kHz / 16-bit minimum (48kHz / 24-bit preferred)
- OGG or WAV format

### Style Reference Playlist

Provide composer with:
1. Devil's Crush - Main Theme
2. Castlevania: Symphony of the Night - Various tracks
3. Doom (2016) - BFG Division (for intensity reference)
4. Shovel Knight - Strike the Earth
5. The Witcher 3 - Combat music

## Sound Effect Production Notes

### Source Strategy

**Primary**: High-quality free sound libraries
- Freesound.org
- OpenGameArt.org
- Sonniss GDC bundles

**Secondary**: Foley recording (future version)
- Metal impacts (swords, shields, armor)
- Organic ogre sounds
- Ice cracking and wind

### Processing

- All sounds should be EQ'd for clarity
- Compression for consistent volume
- No harsh frequencies
- Slight reverb for cohesion (not too wet)

## Budget Considerations

### Minimum Viable Audio

If budget is extremely limited:
1. One main gameplay track (looping)
2. Basic sound effects from free libraries
3. Text-only for "voice" lines (displayed on screen)
4. Simple victory/defeat stings

### Ideal Audio

Full track list, custom sound effects, professional voice acting for Sasha at minimum, enemy voices as stretch goal.

### Stretch Goal Audio

Everything above plus:
- Adaptive music system
- Full enemy voice acting
- Custom foley recording
- Multiple announcer options
