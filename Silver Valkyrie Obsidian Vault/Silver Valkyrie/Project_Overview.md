# Silver Valkyrie - Project Overview

## The Pitch

**Silver Valkyrie** is a pixel art video pinball game in the style of *Devil's Crush* and *Sonic Spinball*—not a realistic pinball simulation, but a *video game* that uses pinball as its core mechanic. Players take on the role of Sasha, the Silver Valkyrie, represented by the ball itself as she battles through Norse mythological horrors across a multi-screen scrolling playfield.

> "Viking lady. Monsters. Pinball."

---

## Core Inspirations

### Primary Gameplay Reference
- **Devil's Crush** (TurboGrafx-16, 1990) - Multi-screen scrolling playfield, animated enemies on the table, dark fantasy aesthetic, bonus rooms
- **Alien Crush** (TurboGrafx-16, 1988) - Biomechanical horror pinball, creatures that react to the ball
- **Sonic Spinball** (Genesis, 1993) - Character-as-ball concept, boss encounters, platformer-pinball hybrid

### Visual & Tonal Reference
- **Heavy Metal magazine** - Bold, unapologetic fantasy art
- **1980s Bally/Williams cabinet art** - *Fathom*, *Centaur*, *Flash Gordon*
- **16-bit pixel art** - Pre-rendered sprite aesthetic (Donkey Kong Country, Baldur's Gate)

### Design Philosophy Reference
- **Monster Bash** (Williams, 1998) - Mode-based progression toward wizard mode
- **Medieval Madness** (Williams, 1997) - Thematic integration of targets and story

---

## What Makes This Different

The pixel art video pinball space is nearly empty. **Demon's Tilt** (2019) revived the Devil's Crush style but uses high-resolution 2D art with a Lovecraftian cosmic horror theme. 

Silver Valkyrie occupies a different niche:
- **Pixel art aesthetic** - Chunky, stylized sprites evoking 16-bit classics
- **Norse mythology** - Vikings, Valkyries, trolls, draugr (not tentacles and tarot)
- **Heavy Metal fantasy** - Bold, campy, unapologetically fun
- **Accessible hardware requirements** - No beefy GPU needed

## Technical Specifications

**Engine**: Unity (2D)  
**Art Style**: Pixel art / pre-rendered sprites  
**Resolution**: TBD (targeting classic 16-bit proportions)  
**Platforms**: PC (Steam, itch.io)  
**Target Price**: $5 USD  

### Art Pipeline
1. 3D models created in Blender (existing assets from earlier development)
2. Rendered through pixel art addon/shader at low resolution
3. Touched up and animated in Aseprite
4. Key character art hand-drawn or adapted from Curt Sibling commissions

## Project History

Silver Valkyrie began as a realistic virtual pinball simulation project in partnership with programmer AntAptive. After that collaboration stalled due to scheduling conflicts, the project pivoted to a Devil's Crush-style video game pinball—a direction that better suits solo/small team development and leverages existing design work.

In January 2026, programmer Jesse (Peacebaby313) joined the project through mutual connection Zayne, bringing Unity C# expertise and enthusiasm for the Sonic Spinball/Devil's Crush style.
